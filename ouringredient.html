OUR VEGETABLES

At Sushi Wara, we care about where our ingredients come from. That’s why we work closely with local farmers to get fresh, seasonal vegetables at their best—full of flavor and nutrition. We even grow some of our own herbs and greens right in our little garden, so we know exactly what’s going into your meal. By using what’s in season, we keep our dishes colorful, flavorful, and in tune with nature—all while doing our part to support the environment.

OUR FISH

We get our fish fresh every day from the clean waters around Vancouver Island, working with local fishermen that share our commitment to sustainability. Every catch is handled with care to keep it as fresh and pure as possible. From the moment it arrives to the moment it hits your plate, we follow strict preparation and safety standards—so you get clean, sashimi-grade seafood that tastes just as amazing as it looks.

OUR DRINKS

  1. Herbs

    A lot of the herbs we use—like mint and shiso—come straight from our little garden behind the kitchen. Growing them ourselves means they’re always fresh, pesticide-free, and full of natural aroma. Before any herb goes into your drink, we give it a good rinse and hand-check it to make sure it’s perfect.

  2. Fruits

    We work with trusted local growers across British Columbia to bring in the best seasonal fruits—always picked for ripeness, flavor, and quality. Each piece is carefully inspected, washed, and prepped fresh every day, so every sip you take is bursting with freshness and handled with care.

  3. Glassware

    We take great care in hand-polishing and sanitizing all our glassware to make sure it’s spotless and hygienic. Whether it’s a chilled martini glass or a frosted highball, every glass is handled with attention to detail, so you can enjoy your drink knowing we’ve prioritized both cleanliness and presentation.
